Available Projects

The following projects are suitable primarily for strong undergraduate graduates, either as Honours Thesis projects or in groups for their design projects, often working in conjunction with graduate students in our lab. Team descriptions are based on the assumption of these being undertaken by undergraduate students. Please read the FAQ and then contact me if you are interested in getting involved in any of these projects.

  • IMAGE
  • CyberSight
  • Speech-to-Vibration
  • AR Art Therapy
  • Navigation Assistance

IMAGE

Making graphics accessible to blind users with audio and haptics. Already deployed as a browser extension and being expanded to mobile.

CyberSight

Helping visually impaired shoppers identify and verify products using AI, computer vision, and smartphones.

Speech-to-Vibration

Turning spoken audio into tactile signals to support communication for deaf and hard-of-hearing individuals.

AR Art Therapy

Helping chronic pain patients express their experience with generative AR tools during therapy sessions.

Navigation Assistance

Smartphone-based vision system to help blind users reach doors, intersections, stairways, and more.
